## System Overview

**Truely2Expose** provides deep-dive analytics that go beyond Google Analytics. It is built for investigators and marketers who need to know the *truth* about their traffic.

### Capabilities

1.  **IP Deanonymization:** Identify the real location of visitors behind VPNs.
2.  **Device Fingerprinting:** Track users across sessions without cookies.
3.  **Spy Tool Detection:** Know when a competitor is ripping your lander.

### Usage

Simply add the tracking pixel to your header.

```html
<script src="https://truely2expose.com/tracker.js" id="t2e-pixel"></script>
```
