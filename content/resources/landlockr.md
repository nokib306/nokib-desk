## System Overview

**LandLockr** is designed to be the ultimate cloud locking mechanism. In the affiliate marketing ecosystem, protecting your landing pages and assets from spy tools and theft is critical.

### Key Features

*   **Asset Protection:** Encrypts your landing page source code.
*   **Link Cloaking:** Hides your affiliate links from ad networks.
*   **Bot Filtering:** Automatically blocks non-human traffic.

### Integration

LandLockr integrates directly with your existing Nginx or Apache server.

```bash
curl -sL https://landlockr.cloud/install.sh | sudo bash
```
